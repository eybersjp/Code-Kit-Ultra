# Objection Handling

## "Isn't this just another coding copilot?"
No. Copilots optimize code generation. Code Kit Ultra governs execution, approval, verification, and auditability.

## "Why not use workflows or CI only?"
Traditional workflows assume deterministic automation. Code Kit Ultra is designed for AI-assisted, adaptive, risk-aware execution.

## "Why does tenant-aware identity matter?"
Because operator trust and enterprise readiness depend on scoping every action to the right actor, org, and project.

## "How do you prevent unsafe autonomy?"
With simulation, risk scoring, approval gates, policy enforcement, bounded adaptations, and audit trails.

## "What is the moat?"
The moat is the governed execution layer: policy, control plane, verification, audit, and the future learning loop.
