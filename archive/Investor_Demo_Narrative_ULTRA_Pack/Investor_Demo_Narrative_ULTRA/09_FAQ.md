# FAQ

## Who is it for?
Engineering teams, platform teams, AI-native builders, and enterprise operators who need trustworthy autonomy.

## What is the first wedge?
Design partners who already use AI coding tools but need safer execution and better governance.

## Why InsForge-first?
Because identity, backend primitives, and session governance need to be core, not bolted on.

## What comes after launch?
Hosted control plane, enterprise isolation options, learning-driven optimization, and commercial packaging.
