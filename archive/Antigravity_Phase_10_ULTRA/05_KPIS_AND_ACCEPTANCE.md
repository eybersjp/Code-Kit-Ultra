# KPIs and Acceptance Criteria

## KPIs
- Retry rate decreases by 20% after 30 days
- Mean verification pass rate improves by 10%
- Mean operator rating >= 4.2 / 5
- High-risk false-positive gate rate reduced by 15% without increasing incident rate
- Adapter reliability score coverage > 90% of active adapters
- Policy adaptation rollback rate < 5%

## Acceptance criteria
- Every completed run can produce an outcome record
- Learning patterns are auditable to source runs
- Reliability scores are visible in control plane
- Optimizer can simulate before apply
- No autonomous relaxation of risk controls without explicit approval
- Reversion path exists for every applied policy change
