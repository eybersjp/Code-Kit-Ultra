# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free environment.

## Expected behavior
- be respectful and constructive
- assume good intent
- focus on ideas, not people
- help keep the project useful and trustworthy

## Unacceptable behavior
- harassment, discrimination, abuse
- doxxing or threats
- intentionally unsafe or malicious contributions

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when needed to protect the community.
